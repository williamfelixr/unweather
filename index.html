william f-r

I am a Manchester-based musician, producer and amateur artist working in a range of genres and media. I release music under the alias Unweather, and have composed music for a range of interactive experiences, including an ongoing collaboration with digital artist Elle Bulger. I play a range of instruments with Manchester-based psych-rock band Farfisa and have also acted as mix engineer for the band's last three singles.

As an instrumentalist I am primarily a bass guitarist, but I also play guitar, synthesizers, and am currently learning the clarinet.

My interests include: cybernetics; generative and stochastic music-making methods; jazz improvisation; JavaScript; mycelium networks; and drawing.
